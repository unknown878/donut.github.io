A doughnut or donut is a type of leavened fried dough. It is popular in many countries and is prepared in various forms as a sweet snack that can be homemade or purchased in bakeries, supermarkets, food stalls, and franchised
specialty vendors.



How to make a doughnut?



  1. Cream the butter and sugar. Start by beating the butter and 1 cup of the sugar in a
    large bowl until the mixture is thick and pale colored, about 5 minutes. ...

  2. Bring the dough together and let it rest. ...

  3. Cut the doughnuts .

  4. Fry 'em up.

  5. Roll the doughnuts in cinnamon and sugar.
